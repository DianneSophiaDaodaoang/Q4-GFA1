FOR LIFE: A Charity for the Needy


For Life is a charity that aims to address the basic needs of people living in poverty. The charity would focus on providing access to food, water, shelter, and healthcare to individuals and families who are homeless. This charity organize fundraising events, seek donations from individuals and corporations, and partner with local businesses and organizations.
